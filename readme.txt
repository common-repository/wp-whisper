== Description ==

* WP Whisper is an <strong>online webmaster's community</strong> where your can share your interesting content to reach <strong>potential audiences</strong> across the world’s while they are reading interesting content.


<h4>Features</h4>
- Grow your traffic and Reach potential audiences across the world’s while they are reading interesting content.
- Share your content into other webmasters websites.
- Before start using Whisper, you need to <strong>register</strong> your web site. Its FREE.
- A Private key will be generated for you, the private key is your ONLY WAY to send any request to our server via our secure api. PLEASE KEEP IT SECURE.
- By using this plugin you agree that, during any interaction with our server, your email or your blog name or your blog url will be sent to our server.
- If you have any questions about our Privacy, please contact us.
	
== Installation == 
* In your Admin, go to menu Plugins > Add
* Search for WP Whisper
* Click to install
* Activate the plugin
* Register your web site using the new Whisper page under Settings menu.
* Go to edit post and use Whisper it button !
